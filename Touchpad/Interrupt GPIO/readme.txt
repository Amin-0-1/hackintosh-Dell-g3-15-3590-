
To run the I2C HID with interrupt GPIO pinning mode (preferred )


1- place SSDT-TPD0.aml in EFI/clover/patched or EFI/OC/ACPI

2- open rename.plist in Xcode or any plist editor and head to ACPI/Patch , then copy the renamed patches and paste them in you config , ACPI/DSDT/Patch in clover & ACPI/Patch in OC

3- restart ♥️
